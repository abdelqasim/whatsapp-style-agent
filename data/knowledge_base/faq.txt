===== Meeting Scheduling Policy =====
Meetings can be booked Monday through Friday between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM.
Each meeting slot is 60 minutes by default unless otherwise specified.
Please request a meeting at least 24 hours in advance.
Meetings can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 2 hours before the start time.
Back-to-back meetings are allowed but a 15-minute buffer is recommended.
Group meetings of more than 5 participants should be booked through the office manager.
